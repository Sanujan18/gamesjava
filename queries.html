FAQs and Queries

Are Digital codes region locked?

Yes, any download games, subscriptions cards or add-ons are region locked. Please refer to products description to make sure you are buying the right product.

What is your warranty period?

Consoles (Hardware), Games (Software), accessories, subscription/gift cards, and digital PC games do not carry any warranty. However, some Accessories & Consoles (Hardware) have different warranty conditions depending on the manufacturer only if mentioned on the products page. In case of a warranty claim, the customer can contact our customer service team. The warranty covers manufacturing defects only. Physical damage is not covered.

If a new version of the game i already purchased has been released, do i need too pay for the new version?

You do not need to buy another copy of the game, we will be happy to email the updated version directly to you. Email us at gamersworld@gmail.com from the email address you used to order the game and we will send you a download link.
