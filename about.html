About us

As a gaming retailer store we launched our store in 2004. The founder of the store is John who is an interested in gaming and a huge game lover. He started his first store in Colombo 2 named Gamers world. In the gap of 2004 till present our store has became more famous and trustable among the customers. As development of business we expanded our business we have two branches in kandy and Galle. What set the shop apart was its commitment to creating a welcoming environment for gamers of all ages to share their love for gaming.Over time, our store evolved to include a wider range of products, from retro games and consoles to the latest releases and exclusive merchandise

From day one, our mission has been to create a welcoming space for gamers of all ages. Over the years, the shop has grown, expanding to three branches across the city.
